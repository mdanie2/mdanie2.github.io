Lighting Ore

Lightning Ore is a dark purple block with lightning streaks running along every side. The Lightning Ore is embedded with yellow lightning crystals.

Lightning Ores spawn naturally in most caves, caverns, and crevices!

Lightning Ore requires a diamond pickaxe to mine it. A Lightning Ore will drop if a Lighning Ore is broken by a diamond pickaxe. If broken by any other tool, the Lightning Ore will drop nothing.

Lighting Ingot

The Lightning Ingot is essential for any lightning-based block, tool, or weapon.

Ligtning Ingots can be obtained by smelting a lighning ore in a furnace.

Furthermore, Thor's Block can be placed on a crafting table for nine Lightning Ingots.

Lighting Sword

The Lightning Sword is one of the strongest weapons in the game! It is stronger and more durable than its diamond counterpart.

Lighning Swords can be constructed with two lightning ingots and a stick.

Thor's Block

Thor's Block exemplifies "Lightning Mod's" electrifying features. It is among the toughest, hardest, most colorful, and most diverse blocks in the game.

Thor's Block can't be found naturally. It must be constructed from nine Lightning Ingots.

Thor's Block is a provider of redstone power! Thor's Block can power redstone components up to 10 blocks away.

Thor's Block will truly light up your world. Use it to illuminate paths or make it easier to see mobs.

Standing on Thor's Block will cause lightning to rain from the heavens. Use it to harm mobs. But be careful: lightning can also strike you!
