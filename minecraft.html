Lightning Mod

The Lightning Mod is a must-have for anyone who loves Minecraft AND lightning. The Lightning Mod introduces new lightning-themed blocks, weapons, items, and mobs. Every new entity has its own unique design, abilities, and attributes.

You can find descriptions and pictures of every item on this webpage. Don't just read about these features, though! Try them out yourself by downloading the Lightning Mod here.

Lightning Ore

Lightning ore is a dark purple block with lightning streaks running along every side. Lightning ores are embedded with yellow crystals.

Lightning ores spawn naturally in most caves, caverns, and crevices!

Lightning ores can only be mined with a diamond pickaxe. If broken by any other tool, the lightning ore will drop nothing.

Lightning Ingot

Lightning ingots are essential for any lightning-based block, tool, and weapon.

Lightning ingots can be obtained by smelting a lighning ore in a furnace.

Furthermore, Thor's Block can be placed on a crafting table to produce nine lightning ingots.

Lightning Sword

The lightning sword is one of the strongest weapons in the game. It deals more damage than its diamond counterpart.

Lighning swords can be constructed with two lightning ingots and a stick.

Thor's Block

Thor's Block exemplifies Lightning Mod electrifying features. It is one of the toughest, hardest, most colorful, and most diverse blocks in the game.

Thor's Block can't be found naturally. It must be constructed from nine lightning ingots.

Thor's Block is a provider of redstone power! Thor's Block can power redstone components up to 10 blocks away.

Thor's Block will truly light up your world. Use it to illuminate paths or make it easier to see mobs.

Standing on Thor's Block will cause lightning to rain from the heavens. Use it to harm mobs, strike trees, or let other players know your position. Be careful! The lightning can also strike you!

Thor's Block can be harvested with a diamond pickaxe.

When Thor's Block is broken by a diamond pickaxe, 0 - 3 items will drop. There are nine different drops: Thor's Block, beacon, Lightning Rod, redstone block, TNT, ender pearl, lightning ingot, emerald, or nothing.

Lightning Rod

The Lightning Rod summons one lightning bolt upon use. Use it to light up mobs, players, villagers, or the environment. The Lightning Rod is immune to damage and, thus, will not break due to wear and tear. The only thing that can destroy the Lightning Rod is lava.

Lightning Rods are constructed from three lightning ingots, two Thor's Blocks, two diamonds, and two TNTs.

Lightning Ore Zombie

Lightning Ore Zombies are a variant of zombie that are stronger and fire-resistant, have more health, and gain health from lightning strikes. Lightning Ore Zombies are also, however, slower than a regular zombie.

Lightning Ore Zombies have three different spawns. They can be summoned from eggs...

Generate naturally in most biomes...

And spawn if you stand on a lightning ore for too long.

When you slay a Lightning Ore Zombie, you will be rewarded with a lightning ingot or rotten flesh.
